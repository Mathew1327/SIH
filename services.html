Service 1

  • Personalized Cognitive Training Plans: Offer personalized cognitive training plans tailored to the specific needs and abilities of each child with disabilities.
  • Assessments: Conduct assessments or evaluations to identify cognitive strengths and weaknesses.
  • Design Programs: Design individualized training programs targeting areas such as attention, memory, executive function, and problem-solving skills.
  • Varied Activities: Provide a variety of cognitive exercises and activities suited to different learning styles and preferences.

Service 2

  • Progress Tracking and Reporting: Implement a system for tracking and monitoring each child's progress in their cognitive training program.
  • Data Collection: Collect data on performance metrics, such as accuracy, response time, and completion rates for cognitive exercises.
  • Generate Reports: Generate regular progress reports to share with parents, caregivers, and healthcare professionals.
  • Insights and Recommendations: Offer insights and recommendations based on progress data to adjust training plans and optimize outcomes.

Service 3

  • Professional Support and Guidance: Provide access to trained professionals, such as cognitive therapists, psychologists, or special education specialists, for guidance and support.
  • Consultations: Offer virtual or in-person consultations to discuss progress, address concerns, and modify training plans as needed.
  • Training Sessions: Conduct training sessions or workshops for parents and caregivers to learn effective strategies for supporting their child's cognitive development at home.
  • Collaboration: Collaborate with schools, clinics, and other community resources to ensure coordinated care and holistic support for children with disabilities.
